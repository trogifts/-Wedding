Our Story

The Scrapbook of Love

September 2024

First Glimpse 🎨

We first met at the Kochi-Muziris Biennale gallery, both standing in front of the same pastel watercolor portrait. A small conversation blossomed into a beautiful friendship.

May 2025

The Sweet Proposal 🌅

Zayn prepared a surprise picnic on the hills of Munnar, proposing with a hand-painted watercolor diary where every page detailed a milestone of our love.

December 2025

The Rings Exchange 💍

Surrounded by our families and soft floral arrangements in Thrissur, we exchanged rings and promised to paint our lives with colors of mutual trust and joy.

September 2026

The Big Day ✨

The union of our hearts, where we take our vows under a canopy of fresh blooms and watercolors, ready to begin our infinite journey.
