Itinerary of the Day

A secular ceremony celebrating companionship, featuring the exchange of fresh jasmine garlands and personal vows with family and friends.

Attire Suggestions

We invite you to dress in light, comfortable festive wear. Traditional Kerala handloom ivory/cream, gold accents, or soft pastel shades are highly welcome.

Important Details

Common Questions

Sadhya is a multi-course vegetarian banquet traditionally served on a fresh banana leaf. It includes over 20 unique curries, pickles, rice, and sweet payasams. We will also have a coastal seafood buffet for guests wanting other local delicacies.

Cochin International Airport (COK) is the closest airport (about 45 minutes from the venue). Taxis and ride-shares are readily available at the terminal.
