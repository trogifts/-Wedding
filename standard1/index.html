First Conversation

It started with a chance encounter in a quiet botanical cafe. Zayn was absorbed in a design schematic, while Aara was sketching architectural column contours. A spilt cup of tea and a shared laugh paved the way for a hours-long talk about geometry, nature, and calligraphy.
